Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 09 July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Doctor Digital Pad, a digital prescription software product by J Suraj Enterprises. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (www.doctordigitalpad.com) or use our mobile and web applications. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal identification information from Users in a variety of ways, including, but not limited to, when Users visit our site, register on the app, fill out a form, and in connection with other activities, services, features, or resources we make available.

  • Doctor/Clinic Information: Name, email address, phone number, clinic address, and medical registration number required for account setup and prescription generation.
  • Patient Information: To facilitate digital prescriptions, you may input patient data (such as name, age, gender, contact number, and medical history). We process this data strictly to provide the service and generate prescriptions or WhatsApp/SMS notifications.

3. How We Use Collected Information

J Suraj Enterprises uses the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To personalize user experience and improve our software platform.
  • To send periodic emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages regarding account updates, service changes, or tech support.
  • To facilitate the delivery of digital prescriptions to patients via our integrated third-party APIs (like WhatsApp Business API and SMS gateways).
  • To maintain legal and regulatory compliance.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information with our business partners.

We use third-party service providers (such as Meta for WhatsApp messaging and MSG91 for SMS delivery) to help us operate our business. These third parties have access to your personal information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

5. Data Security

We adopt appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information, username, password, transaction information, and data stored on our platform.
