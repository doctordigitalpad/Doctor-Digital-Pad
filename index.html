Your Data, Secured & Respected.

We understand the sensitivity of healthcare information. Doctor Digital Pad is built on enterprise-grade architecture, ensuring that both clinic and patient data are handled with the highest level of security.

  • What Data We Store

    We safely store only essential text data: Patient names, vitals, prescribed medicines, and the doctor's custom medicine list.

  • World-Class Servers

    All text data is encrypted and hosted on Google's Firebase Firestore infrastructure, ensuring 99.9% uptime and zero data loss.

  • Zero PDF Storage Policy

    To ensure privacy, PDFs are generated locally, sent securely via WhatsApp, and instantly wiped from our temporary servers. We do not store your prescription files.
